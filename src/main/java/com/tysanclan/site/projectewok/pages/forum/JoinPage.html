Joining the Tysan Clan is in itself a simple process, though quite different from that of many other clans. The process takes approximately 3 days, during which you may also withdraw your application. If you cannot wait 3 days

Application

To start the join process, you must first submit an application. This application will be publicly visible, and consists of a short motivation of why you wish to join.

Mentor

Once your application has been sent, another member must agree to become your mentor. If no mentor volunteers within three days, your application will be rejected.

Approval

Once you have a mentor, the Senate must approve your application. A Senator can vote either in your favor or against you. If at least 66% of the voting Senators vote in your favor, you will be granted a trial membership. If no Senators vote (which is rare, but possible), you will also be granted a trial membership.

Proceed

If you understand the above procedure, you may continue the join procedure. You have already applied to join Tysan, please visit your Join Thread.
