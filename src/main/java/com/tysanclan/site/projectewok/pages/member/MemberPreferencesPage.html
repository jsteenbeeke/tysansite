Any member who has been with the clan for two years or longer has the option to become a retired member, under a number of conditions:
  • The member in question holds the rank of Senior Member or Revered Member (and therefore, holds no position as Chancellor, Senator or Truthsayer)
  • The member in question is not the leader of any gaming group
Becoming a retired member has the following consequences:
  • A retired member may not create gaming groups, nor lead them - though he is free to join them
  • A retired member can not be elected as Chancellor or Senator (nor can he receive nominations for these positions), nor be appointed as Truthsayer
  • A retired member will not have his membership terminated, unless he does not log in for a full year
You do not qualify to become a retired member

You are currently a retired member. If you wish to end your retired status, please proceed by clicking the button labelled Cease.

You are currently eligible to become a retired member. If you wish to become a retired member, please click the button labelled Retire. Note that your retirement can be revoked at any time if you so choose.

When going away for an extended period of time (more than 14 days), it is recommended to activate Vacation Mode. Normally, your membership is terminated if you do not log in once every 14 days. When vacation mode is active, this period is extended to 60 days, but only if you do not log in during your vacation!

Since you are currently in your trial period, please consider the following: why did you join Tysan if you knew you wouldn't be around during your trial period. Having Vacation mode active during your trial period will negatively affect your acceptance vote.

To activate vacation mode, press the Activate button below. BEWARE! PUSHING THIS BUTTON WILL LOG YOU OUT, AND THE SECOND YOU LOG IN AGAIN YOUR VACATION MODE WILL BE DEACTIVATED!

We understand that we can not keep everybody happy, and that at times people will choose to go their own way. If you no longer wish to remain a member of the Tysan Clan, you can use the button below to terminate your membership. Once you have pressed the button, your account will be turned back into a regular forum account.

Whatever your reasons are for leaving, we would like that you share them with us. After you have terminated your membership, please make a forum thread to explain your reasons for leaving.
