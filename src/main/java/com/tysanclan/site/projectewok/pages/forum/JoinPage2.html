To join Tysan, you need to write a short motivation about why you wish to join. This motivation should be at least 75 words. If you are unsure what to put here, tell us something about the following:
  • What games do you play, and what you enjoy about them?
  • What kind of person are you?
  • How long have you been playing online?
  • What are you looking for in a clan?

In addition, we would like to know what your primary game is. This will make it easier for us to find a suitable mentor.
